Configure github SSH keys

ssh-keygen -t rsa -b 4096 -C "your_email@example.com" cat ~/.ssh/id_rsa.pub Copy the contents of ~/.ssh/id_rsa.pub to your clipboard Login to your account on https://github.com Go to https://github.com/settings/keys Click the New SSH Key button, add a title, and then paste your public key Click the Add SSH Key button
